About

I have had the opportunity to work with many multi-departmental teams, spread across countries and time zones. I love collaborating with passionate people to make a vision become reality, and appreciate the Agile methodology to help us work quickly and efficiently. As a developer with a UI/UX focus, my goal is a product that is simple, intuitive and accessible. I love talking with users to get an understanding of their workflow and pain points.
